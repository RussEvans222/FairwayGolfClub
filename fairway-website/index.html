The Arrival

Walk in. Grab a bay. Play.

Check in at the kiosk, grab your clubs, and you're on the tee in under two minutes. No waiting on staff, no fumbling with gear you don't own. Just golf.

For Groups

The best way to spend a Friday night.

Book a bay for four, order a round, and compete on courses you'll never play in real life. Fairway is built for the moments that turn into stories.

The Bigger Picture

One club. Proven. Then everywhere.

Northern Virginia is the proof of concept. If the numbers hold — and the model is built to make them hold — Fairway Golf Club becomes a replicable franchise. Every market with golfers, no indoor competition, and a strong median income is a candidate. We build one right, then we build many.
