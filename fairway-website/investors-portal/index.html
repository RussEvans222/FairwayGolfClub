Risk Mitigation

Six layers of protection.

  • Founder-operated — hands-on management from day one, deeply invested in success.
  • Cash-flow positive in 3 months — the business feeds itself immediately.
  • Equipment-secured debt — simulators are resalable assets (~$40K recovery).
  • Automated operations — 2 part-time staff, not 10. Technology replaces headcount.
  • Local ownership — founder lives minutes from the location, not an absentee operator.
  • Worst case — sell equipment, exit lease. Net loss ~$35K split across investors. Painful, not devastating.
