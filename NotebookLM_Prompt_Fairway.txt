WHAT THIS IS:
Fairway Golf Club is the first AI-powered indoor golf improvement club. It's not another dark simulator rental. It's a premium members' club where technology remembers every shot, coaches you between visits, and builds a community of golfers who actually get better.

LOCATION:
Workhouse Arts Center, Lorton, Virginia. Historic red brick campus. Brewery next door (Bunny Man Brewing). Affluent area. Zero indoor golf competition within 15 minutes.

THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE:
Indoor golf simulators exist — but they're dark rooms where you rent a bay, hit balls for an hour, and leave with nothing. No data. No coaching. No community. No reason to come back other than bad weather. It's the golf equivalent of a batting cage.

THE SOLUTION:
Fairway Golf Club creates a "Golfer360" digital profile for every member. Every shot you hit — launch angle, ball speed, spin rate, carry distance — is captured automatically. Our AI coach (built on Salesforce Agentforce) analyzes your data and creates personalized improvement plans. When you come back next week, it remembers. "Hey Russ — driver accuracy was up 12% last time. Today we're working on your 7-iron. You're 8 yards short of target."

Every visit makes you a better golfer. That's not a slogan — it's the technology.

THE EXPERIENCE (walk through a visit):
1. You pull up to a beautiful red brick building. Gold lettering. Warm light through glass. This isn't a warehouse.
2. You check in on an iPad kiosk. "Welcome back — Bay 2 is ready." No waiting.
3. The bay monitor greets you with today's plan based on your last session.
4. You hit balls. Every shot is tracked. The AI adjusts in real time.
5. Between sessions, you grab a craft beer from the bar (Bunny Man Brewing on tap, literally brewed 50 feet away).
6. The lounge has leather chairs, golf books, a leaderboard on the TV. People come early. People stay late.
7. On your way out, you grab a branded headcover. Because you're a member now.
